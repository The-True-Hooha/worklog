About Worklog

Worklog is a lightweight activity log for tracking your work time. When you're working, keep this tab open.

Every full interval (default 60 minutes, configurable), you will hear a beep and a modal will ask for a short note on what you worked on. Press Send to save it locally. If you forgot the tab open, press Erase to discard that time.

You can pause/resume at any time. Closing the tab ends the session.

🔒 Local Mode: All your logs are stored in your browser's local storage. No data is sent to any server.

✨ Features:

  • Statistics: View your work patterns and totals
  • Export: Download your logs as JSON or CSV
  • Import: Restore logs from a previous export
  • Timeline: Visualize your work hours over time

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts:

  • ? - Toggle interval control visibility
  • Space - Pause/Resume timer
  • A - Add log entry (independent of timer)
  • L - Log current elapsed time (resets timer)
  • S - Show statistics
  • R - View reports (daily/weekly/monthly)
  • E - Export/Import data
  • C - Settings (includes Pomodoro)
  • M - Manage projects
